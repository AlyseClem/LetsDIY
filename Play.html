How to Make a Bubble Wand and Bubble Mix!



Materials Needed:

  • A small bowl
  • 5 mL (1 teaspoon) of liquid dish soap
  • 30 mL (1/8 cup) of water
  • 30 cm of beading wire


Instructions
Step 1: Pour water into small bowl.
Step 2: Mix in liquid soap.
Step 3: Cut beading wire into a 30 cm piece.
Step 4: Take top half of wire and fold it along the bottom half to form a loop.
Step 5: Twist the wire around itself a few times to ensure it will not come apart. This is what the wire should look like.
Step 6: Dip the wand into the mix and fully submerge the loop before drawing it out. Now you have a bubble wand!
