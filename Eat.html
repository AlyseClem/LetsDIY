How to Flavor Lemonade!

We'll teach you how to flavor lemonade in 3 different ways!



Method 1 - Flavored Syrup

  1. Pour 2 cups of lemonade into a tall glass
  2. Take 2 teaspoons of your favorite flavored syrup (we used grape) and mix it into the glass
  3. You can add more lemonade or flavored syrup to taste
  4. Stir and enjoy!

Method 2 - Powdered Drink Mix

  1. Pour 2 cups of lemonade into a tall glass
  2. Using a small powdered drink mix (we used fruit punch), pour 1/4 teaspoon of powder into the lemonade
  3. Stir and enjoy!

Method 3 - Coconut Cream

  1. Pour 2 cups of lemonade into a tall glass
  2. Pour 1 teaspoon of cream of coconut into the glass
  3. Stir thoroughly and enjoy!
